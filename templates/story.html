A new deadly disease has quickly spread across Earth, threatened to destroy civilization.
Scientists has built a safe zone in Helsinki Vantaa Airport that protects people from the disease.
You were a pilot for Finnair before the disease, now you will fly to rescue people worldwide.

Your mission is to rescue 100 people from at least 5 countries.
You have some money and fuel at first, and you earn 50 money per person you rescue.
Remember to refuel before taking off and be mindful of the plane capacity for passengers.
If you rescue more than 150 people, the plane will crash.

Each airport has their own fuel price, so remember to buy more when fuel is cheap.
Each airport has 25% chance to get buffs or debuffs.
You can gain or lose money, or change how efficiently your plane uses fuel.
1 fuel travels 0.8 kilometer when fuel efficiency is 0.8.

Good luck and have fun!
